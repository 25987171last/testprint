About Us

The National Youth Ministry of the Wesleyan Church of Papua New Guinea is dedicated to empowering young people to grow spiritually, develop leadership skills, and impact their communities for Christ. We serve youth across the nation through discipleship, fellowship, and mission outreach.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To raise a generation of Christ-centered young leaders who transform Papua New Guinea through faith, integrity, and service.

Mission

To disciple youth through biblical teaching, leadership development, evangelism, and community outreach programs.

Youth Programs

Discipleship Training

Weekly Bible studies and mentorship programs to strengthen faith and character.

Leadership Workshops

Training young leaders for service within the church and their communities.

Community Outreach

Evangelism, mission trips, and community service initiatives across PNG.
